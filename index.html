Build Resilience in Your Child and Help Prevent Bullying with the Bullying Prevention App

Research shows that spending at least 15 minutes a day speaking with your child can build the foundation for a strong relationship and provide reassurance that they can come to you if they have a problem. Take a few minutes and “check in,” by asking about school, their friends, and any challenges they face. SAMHSA’s Bullying Prevention App has simple conversation starters to begin a dialogue with your child.

App Features

  • Learn about bullying and how to recognize warning signs your child may be bullying, being bullied, or being a bystander to bullying.
  • Talk with your child about bullying and bullying prevention with easy-to-use conversation starters.
  • Set reminders to talk with your child at times that work best for you and your family.
  • Share conversation tips and advice right from the app via Facebook, Twitter, email and text message.
  • Access special section for educators.
